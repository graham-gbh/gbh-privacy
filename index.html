Privacy Policy

GBH Management LLC · Last updated: April 29, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how GBH Management LLC ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you interact with us, including through our SMS text messaging program for appointment scheduling and lead inquiries related to manufactured home communities.

Information We Collect

When you respond to one of our advertisements (for example, on Zillow, Facebook Marketplace, or other listing platforms) or otherwise engage with our SMS program, we may collect the following information:

  • Your name
  • Your mobile phone number
  • Your email address (if provided)
  • The community or property you inquired about
  • Your scheduling preferences and appointment details
  • The content of messages you send to us

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to:

  • Respond to your inquiry about available homes or communities
  • Schedule appointments and tours
  • Send you transactional messages related to your inquiry or appointment
  • Provide customer service and follow up on your interest
  • Comply with applicable laws and regulations

SMS Messaging Program

By providing your mobile phone number through one of our advertisements, lead forms, or by replying to one of our messages, you consent to receive SMS text messages from GBH Management LLC related to your inquiry, including appointment scheduling and follow-up.

Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. You can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message. For help, reply HELP.

No Sharing or Sale of Mobile Information

We do not sell, rent, or share your mobile phone number or SMS opt-in data with any third parties for their marketing or promotional purposes. Mobile information collected through our SMS program — including phone numbers and consent — will not be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing purposes under any circumstances.

Service Providers

We may share limited information with trusted service providers who help us operate our business and SMS program — for example, our messaging platform, scheduling tools, and customer relationship management software. These providers are contractually obligated to protect your information and use it only to provide services to us. They are not permitted to use your information for their own marketing purposes.

Legal Disclosures

We may disclose information when required by law, subpoena, or other legal process, or when we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, protect your safety or the safety of others, investigate fraud, or respond to a government request.

Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect the information we collect. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as reasonably necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

Your Choices

  • Opt out of SMS: Reply STOP to any message at any time.
  • Access or delete your information: You may request access to or deletion of the information we hold about you by contacting us using the information below.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Material changes will be communicated through reasonable means.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our SMS program, or if you would like to exercise any of your rights, please contact us through the contact form on the property listing or community where you submitted your inquiry, or reach out to the leasing or sales office for that community directly.
