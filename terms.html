Program Description

Program name: GBH Tour Scheduling.

Purpose: The Program helps prospective residents who have inquired about a manufactured home at one of our communities get information about that home (such as lot rent, floor plans, and move-in costs), schedule an in-person tour, receive tour reminders, and get answers to their questions. Messages are sent by Lisa, our AI assistant, on behalf of the community you inquired about.

Message frequency: Message frequency varies. Participants typically receive approximately 3 to 8 messages over a 21-day period, ending when you complete or cancel your scheduled tour, opt out, or go inactive.

Cost: Message and data rates may apply, depending on your mobile phone service plan. We do not charge a separate fee for the Program.
